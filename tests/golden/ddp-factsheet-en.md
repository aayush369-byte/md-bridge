### Holistic is our way




## Factsheet

Foundation Level

### Target group

The Digital Design Professional is addressed to professionals from Requirements Engineering, business analysis, CX & UX, project and produc t management. Therefore, to all roles that work in areas where digital product s, systems, and services are designed and improved.

### Prerequisites

None

### Contents

The Digital Design Professional offers a broad overview of the competence areas for anyone who wants to design future-proof digital solutions. This involves visions and business models, user interfaces and technical interfaces, perceivable user-centered functions and qualities as well as underlying technical functions, data structures and qualities.

### The content is based on the three competence areas:

### Design competence

integration of Digital Design into the building process conceptual work in Digital Design application of prototypes in Digital Design **Material competence** understanding technology and its relation to quality perceivable and  underlying technologies the design perspective on technology **Cross-cutting competence** human factors in Digital Design business models for digital solutions people management & team composition

### Exam

60-minute multiple-choice test
